About this guide

This page explains who the guide is for, what it covers, and how to use the notes page and downloadable brochure.

Download brochure

  2. About this guide

Purpose

This guide is designed to help readers understand heirs’ property, identify urgent issues, organize records, and prepare for more informed conversations with support providers.

Audience

The intended audience includes South Carolina families dealing with heirs’ property, title confusion, tax notices, family conflict around land, or uncertainty after a relative’s death.

Scope

The guide emphasizes education, structure, and preparation. It is not legal advice and does not replace case-specific review.

The site includes a notes page for device-local recordkeeping and a downloadable brochure PDF for offline use.
