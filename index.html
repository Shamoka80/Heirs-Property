Trust and scope

This is an educational resource, not legal advice. It is designed to help families prepare, organize information, and understand next steps before talking with a lawyer, legal aid office, or trusted community organization.

Who this site is for

This guide is for families in South Carolina dealing with shared family land, unclear title after a death, tax pressure, or disagreements about land decisions. It is written for people under stress who need clear, calm, practical direction.

Need help now?

For heirs’ property assistance in South Carolina, contact the Center for Heirs’ Property at (843) 745-7055 or toll-free at (866) 657-2676. For civil legal-aid intake, contact South Carolina Legal Services at (888) 346-5592.

This guide is educational information, not legal advice. Laws, court procedures, agency rules, and program eligibility can change. Confirm details with an attorney, legal aid office, or official agency before acting.
